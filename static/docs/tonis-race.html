Strollin’ For The Colon and the 5K walk/run began in 2012 to raise funds to support those who are fighting colorectal cancer. Toni’s Race is tentatively scheduled for Fall 2021 at Mendon Ponds Park Stuart Lodge. Stay tuned for more information about our 2021 event and registration.

Toni's Race

Due to the Covid-19 crisis, and the restrictions from all local and state officials, our 9th annual Toni’s Race scheduled for May 9th, 2020 was cancelled. This space will be updated as we determine when it will be safe to hold our event. Currently, we are hoping to schedule our 10th Annual Toni’s Race in the fall 2021.

Thank you for your support and understanding. Contact us via e-mail at StrollinfortheColon5k@gmail.com with any questions or concerns. We remain dedicated to the health and wellness of our local community. Please stay safe and healthy!
