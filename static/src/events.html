Dress In Blue

The month of March is National Colorectal Cancer Awareness Month. Strollin’ for the Colon and the University of Rochester Medical Center’s Gastroenterology Division hosted the annual event on Friday, March 5, 2021. Dress in Blue Day brings awareness to colorectal cancer and honors all who are impacted by this disease. By wearing blue, participants can join us to promote our message that all men and women age 50 and older should be screened for colorectal cancer. We encourage everyone to wear blue to show your support. Post a photo of yourself in blue to the Strollin for the Colon Facebook page to show your support.
